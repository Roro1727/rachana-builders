Company Profile

A premier name in Bhavnagar real estate

Established in 2017, Rachana Builders is a premier real estate company with its main focus on providing projects of the highest standards of quality.

A RERA-registered partnership firm based on Waghawadi Road, we build each home painstakingly — with quality, useful detailing and true value for money. Our Victoria collection spans residential and commercial landmarks across Bhavnagar.
